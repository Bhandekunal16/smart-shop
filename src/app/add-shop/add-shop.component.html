Quick tips

To add shop details to your dashboard, you will need the following information.

  • Shop Name: Name of the shop.
  • Address: Full address of the shop excluding the pin number.
  • Official Email: Email address designated for official communications related to the shop.
  • Official Contact Number: Contact number designated for official communications related to the shop.
  • Shop GST: GST number assigned to the shop.
  • PAN Card: PAN card number of the shop owner or the shop itself.
  • Pin Code: Postal code of the shop location.
